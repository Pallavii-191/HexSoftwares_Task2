Our Mission

Paws & Compassion is dedicated to providing shelter, medical care, and loving temporary homes for abandoned, abused, and neglected animals. We believe every life is valuable, and we work tirelessly to advocate for animal welfare, promote responsible pet ownership, and achieve a world without animal cruelty.

Since our founding in 2015, we've successfully rehomed over 5,000 animals, saving them from difficult situations and pairing them with caring families. Your support makes this crucial work possible every single day.

Our Programs

Our commitment extends across the entire lifecycle of animal rescue and care.

Rescue & Rehabilitation

We operate 24/7 rescue teams and provide critical medical treatment, vaccinations, and recovery support for injured and sick animals.

Adoption Services

A rigorous screening and matching process ensures our animals find safe, permanent, and loving homes. View our adoptable pets online!

Spay/Neuter Initiative

We run low-cost and free spay/neuter clinics to combat pet overpopulation and reduce the number of animals entering shelters.
